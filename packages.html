Our Packages

Buffet

This is a self service option in which allows the guests to serve themselves and choose from a variety of dishes. We offer a wide rage of African cuisines from the different parts of Africa. This option also has an accommodation for those with dietary restrictions eg: gluten free.

Plate Service

This is a more formal option in which the guests are served a selected meal. We offer a variety of menus in which he host chooses which one will be served. This also does not favor people on dietary if not communicated early.

Family-style Service

This a communal dinning option where guests share dishes served in large bowls or platters. This was greatly practiced by the Swahili as it is a more casual gathering that allows guests to try a variety of dishes.
